## Style Prompt

This intro film should feel like entering a private Counter-Strike exhibition after hours: architectural, expensive, cold, and ceremonial. Use a near-black gallery space, stark white type, restrained blood-red scanning light, and a single hero object placed dead-center with a deliberate reveal. Motion should feel engineered rather than flashy: shutters open, the object settles, a scan beam passes, and labels appear like museum metadata.

## Colors

- `#050505` canvas black
- `#141414` carbon panel
- `#f3efe7` exhibition white
- `#b5121b` scan red
- `#d8cfc1` warm ivory accent

## Typography

- `Fraunces` for display statements
- `IBM Plex Mono` for technical labels and metadata

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use neon cyan, purple, or gaming-rig gradients
- Do not place the hero object off-center or undersized
- Do not use bouncy or playful motion
- Do not fill the frame with card UI or dashboard chrome
- Do not rely on blur-heavy glow that hurts clarity
